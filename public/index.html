About

On March 3rd-4th, 2018, over 200 high school students from across the Chicagoland area will come to Maine South High School for 24 hours of designing, making, and hacking. The only limitation is what you want to create! This will be our fourth annual hackathon at Maine South, and we've seen all types of projects, from virtual reality games to machine learning to wooden catapults.

Hack Ridge is the place for anyone interested in software or hardware, whether you have three minutes of experience or three years. Through workshops with industry professionals and mentorships from computer science majors, we guarantee that you will leave with a project created, whatever your idea may be. Plus, you will have a ton of fun doing so!

Admission, food, and swag for the entire 24 hours is free: apply today!
